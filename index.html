02 — Two services. One bar.

Pick your protection.

Two completely different buyer paths. PPF protects your factory paint physically. Ceramic protects it chemically. Most detail shops blur the two; we don’t.

Premium · PPF

Paint Protection Film

Self-healing urethane film over factory paint. Stops rock chips, road debris, and chemical etching. Full front, full body, or track-pack coverage.

Premium · Ceramic

Ceramic Coating

9H-grade nano-ceramic over corrected paint. Hydrophobic, UV-resistant, gloss-amplifying. 5- and 10-year warranty options.

Foundation

Paint Correction

Multi-stage compounding + polishing. Removes swirl marks, oxidation, deep scratches. Required prep step for ceramic + PPF.

Full Service

Concours Detail

Show-grade interior + exterior, every panel, every seam. The detail level our Kings athlete clients book before media days.

04 — The Bay Method

Four phases. One bay door, no hand-offs.

Your car doesn’t move between technicians. One installer, one bay, one project — from the inspection through the final inspection.

01

Inspection

Paint-depth gauge readings, swirl-mark mapping, panel-by-panel check. We tell you what your car actually needs and what it doesn’t.

90 min · on-site
02

Correction

Multi-stage compounding + polishing. The prep most shops skip. PPF and ceramic only work on a properly corrected substrate.

1-3 days
03

Install

PPF or ceramic. Climate-controlled bay, dust-free environment, same installer start-to-finish. Photographed every step.

2-7 days by scope

Final QC

Light-tunnel inspection. Hydrophobic test for ceramic. Edge-seal check for PPF. Walk-around with you before keys back.
